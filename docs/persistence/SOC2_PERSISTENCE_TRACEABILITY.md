# SOC 2 Persistence Traceability

## Purpose

Phase 11 maps persistence foundations to SOC 2-aligned readiness evidence.

This document does not claim SOC 2 certification or operating effectiveness.

## Security

Persistent records prepare the platform for RBAC, tenant boundaries, reviewer permissions, and audit logging.

## Availability

Durable run records and regression baselines support rollback, release gates, and operational continuity evidence.

## Processing Integrity

Evaluation runs, benchmarks, scores, policy decisions, and evidence packages provide a durable basis for reconstructing evaluation outcomes.

## Confidentiality

Evidence package records create the future control point for access restrictions, sensitive context handling, and evidence minimization.

## Privacy

Tenant-scoped records and future retention controls support privacy boundary enforcement once TRUE_MODE is implemented.

## Phase 11 Boundary

Phase 11 provides persistence foundation only. It does not provide final SOC 2 control implementation.
